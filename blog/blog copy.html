Audience Testing

The three task I had for the audience was to just click on the "Get Started" button for "Find a Coach", not click on the "login" button, and just to enter everything in the form. For audience testing, I was able to get good feedback on improvements I could make on my project. These feedbacks includes: including more than 2 gender preferences, include the dots for password, fix my typos, add a profile picture for the coach, contact button to bring to the user's email, and give users an option to choose more than one option.
